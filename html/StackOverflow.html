Signing up for a Stack Overflow

Stack Overflow is an important resource for research and general knowledge of computer science and it is important that we fully utilize it by creating an account.

  • To start, follow this link and navigate to the blue sign up button.
  • Next, choose a display name(similar to GitHub, choose something appropriate) and then signup using an email and password.
